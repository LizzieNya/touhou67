# Touhou 6 Clone - Walkthrough

## Controls
- **Arrow Keys**: Move Character
- **Z**: Shoot / Confirm
- **X**: Bomb (Spell Card) / Cancel
- **Shift**: Focus Mode (Slow movement + Hitbox display)
- **Esc**: Pause Game

## Gameplay Features
- **6 Stages + Extra Stage**: Play through the full story mode or challenge the Extra stage.
- **Boss Battles**: Multi-phase boss fights with Spell Cards.
- **Scoring**: Graze bullets (get close without hitting) and collect items for points.
- **Power Up**: Collect Red 'P' items to increase power (visual only for now).
- **Bombs**: Use 'X' to clear the screen and deal massive damage if you're in a pinch.

## Debug / Cheats
- The game runs at a fixed 60 FPS.
- Collision detection is precise (hitbox is the small white dot when focused).
- You have 3 lives and 3 bombs per life.
