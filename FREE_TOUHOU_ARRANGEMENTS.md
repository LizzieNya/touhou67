# Free Touhou Arrangements for Fan Games

## How Other Fan Games Do It

Most Touhou fan games use **free arrangements** from the community. Here's where to get them:

## Best Sources for FREE Touhou Arrangements

### 1. Touhou Arrange Extended (YouTube)

**What it is**: Channel with extended/looped Touhou arrangements
**License**: Many allow use with credit
**How to use**:
1. Search "Touhou 6 arrange extended"
2. Check video description for usage rights
3. Download with yt-dlp
4. Give credit in your game

**Example searches**:
- "U.N. Owen Was Her arrange extended"
- "Septette for the Dead Princess arrange"
- "Beloved Tomboyish Girl arrange"

### 2. Newgrounds Audio Portal

**Link**: https://www.newgrounds.com/audio

**How to find**:
1. Search each song name
2. Filter by "Use in games: Allowed"
3. Download directly
4. Credit the artist

**Specific searches**:
```
- "U.N. Owen Was Her"
- "Septette for the Dead Princess"  
- "Beloved Tomboyish Girl"
- "Night of Nights" (Sakuya arrange)
```

## Alternative: MIDI Files

**Easiest option**:
1. Search "Touhou 6 MIDI files"
2. Download the MIDIs (tiny files, ~50KB)
3. Use a soundfont to play them
4. MIDIs are recreations, not copies
5. Generally okay to use

**Where to find**:
- "Touhou MIDI collection"
- VGMusic.com
- Various Touhou fan sites
