# Touhou: Nocturnal Sunlight (東方夜光)

## Story
Why is the sun shining at midnight? An unnatural phenomenon has struck Gensokyo - the sun refuses to set, casting eternal daylight even in the dead of night. The intense solar energy is disrupting the balance of day and night, causing chaos for creatures of darkness and throwing nature into disorder.

Journey through 6 stages to uncover the source of this anomaly and restore the natural cycle of day and night!

## Playable Characters

### Main Characters
The Scarlet Devil sisters are the primary protagonists - after all, eternal sunlight is their worst nightmare!

- **Remilia Scarlet** - The Scarlet Devil herself, furious that someone dares bring the sun into HER night
- **Flandre Scarlet** - The basement-dwelling destroyer, finally has a "sun-free" excuse to go outside... except there IS a sun!

### Supporting Cast
- **Sakuya Izayoi** - Perfect and elegant maid, serving her mistress by investigating
- **Youmu Konpaku** - Half-phantom gardener, concerned about the restless spirits
- **Sanae Kochiya** - Living god's wind priestess

### Extra Characters (Callbacks to Classic Touhou)
- **Reimu Hakurei** - Shrine maiden of paradise, investigating like usual
- **Marisa Kirisame** - Ordinary magician, just here for the chaos

## Stage Progression

### Stage 1: The Sun at Midnight
**Boss: Lumina** - A bright fairy reveling in the eternal daylight
Spell Cards:
- Light Sign "Eternal Radiance"
- Sun Sign "Blazing Noon"

### Stage 2-5: [Various mid-bosses and challenges]
Navigate through progressively difficult patterns as you approach the source.

### Stage 6: The Source of Anomaly
**Final Boss: Solstice** - The embodiment of day and night balance
Spell Cards:
- Sun Sign "Solar Flare"
- Moon Sign "Lunar Tide"
- Equinox "Perfect Balance"
- Solstice "Day and Night"

## Features
- 6 full stages with unique enemies and patterns
- Character-specific dialogue for all 7 playable characters
- Epic boss battles with multiple spell card phases
- Beautiful bullet patterns themed around light and darkness
- Challenging difficulty curve suitable for experienced players

## Tips
- **Play as the vampires first!** Remilia and Flandre have the most personal stake in this incident - eternal sunlight is literally deadly to them
- Remilia's Gungnir attack excels at boss damage
- Flandre's destructive power can clear difficult patterns
- Reimu and Marisa are available as "guest star" characters for classic Touhou fans
- Use character-specific shot types to your advantage
- Boss spell cards are more difficult but award more points
- Remember to graze bullets for extra points!
