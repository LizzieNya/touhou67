# Image Generation Prompts for Touhou Clone

Use these prompts in your preferred AI image generator (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3, etc.) to create assets for the game.

## General Style Notes
*   **Dialogue Portraits**: Anime style, waist-up or bust-up, expressive, transparent background (if possible, otherwise remove background later). ZUN-style or modern anime style.
*   **In-Game Sprites**: Top-down pixel art, 32x48 or 48x64 resolution, clear distinction between character and background.
