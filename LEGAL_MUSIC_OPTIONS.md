## Best Sources for Legal Touhou Music

### 1. Free Music Archive (FMA)
**Link**: https://freemusicarchive.org

Search for:
- "touhou style"
- "bullet hell music"
- "doujin music"

**License**: Creative Commons (free to use with credit)

### 2. Incompetech by Kevin MacLeod
**Link**: https://incompetech.com

Search for:
- "Boss Battle"
- "Epic"
- "Fast Piano"

**License**: Free with attribution
**Perfect for**: Boss themes

### 3. OpenGameArt.org
**Link**: https://opengameart.org

Search:
- "boss music"
- "retro game music"
- "chiptune boss"

**License**: Various (check each, most are CC0/CC-BY)
