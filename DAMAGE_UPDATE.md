## Balance Changes

### Why These Changes?

1. **Boss HP Increased**: Bosses now have much more HP to match EoSD
2. **Player Damage Increased**: To compensate and maintain similar clear times
3. **Focused Damage Bonus**: Rewards precise play
4. **Flandre Difficulty**: Extra boss is now truly challenging

### Difficulty Curve

The game now follows authentic Touhou 6 difficulty:
- Early bosses (Rumia, Cirno) are manageable
- Mid-game bosses (Meiling, Patchouli) require skill
- Late-game bosses (Sakuya, Remilia) are challenging
- Extra boss (Flandre) is EXTREME
